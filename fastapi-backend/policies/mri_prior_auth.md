# MRI & Advanced Imaging Prior Authorization

For MRIs and other advanced imaging (CT, PET), prior authorization is required when:
- The billed amount exceeds 15000 (local currency), or
- The procedure is elective and not emergent, or
- The member has already had similar imaging in the last 6 months.

Common denial reasons:
- No prior authorization on file for high-cost imaging.
- Insufficient clinical documentation to justify the MRI.
